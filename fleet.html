Our Vessels

The Auke Bay Fleet

Built for the Inside Passage

Four Vessels.
One Unforgettable Region.

Our fleet spans from nimble day-boats to fully-equipped multi-day Nordic Tugs. Every vessel is USCG-compliant, outfitted with modern electronics, safety gear, and the comforts you need to focus entirely on your adventure.

All boats are available for bareboat charter (self-captained, Alaska boating experience required) or with the addition of a licensed skippered captain at $650/day.
