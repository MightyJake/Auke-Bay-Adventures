Our Story

About Auke Bay Adventures

Who We Are

The Adventure Starts Here

Welcome to Auke Bay Adventures, based in Juneau, Alaska. We hold the keys and the expertise for you to launch the Alaska adventure of a lifetime aboard a private luxury yacht.

Your private party charter itinerary is determined entirely by you as you cruise the calm waters of the Inside Passage of scenic Southeast Alaska. Choose your adventure — the Inside Passage provides an abundance of choices, from whale watching to world-class sport fishing, science and biology charters, kayaking, Glacier Bay and village explorations, photography, and diving.

If you enjoy the ocean, whales, nature on its own terms, and some of the finest fishing you will ever experience, you owe it to yourself to experience it from the deck of a private yacht where the only clock is the midnight sun.

Auke Bay Adventures provides both bareboat and skippered charters on Nordic Tugs and Cutwater yachts — all out of the beautiful Auke Bay Harbor in Juneau, Alaska.

Our Captain

Meet Steve Birkinbine

Steve Birkinbine

Founder & Head Captain — Auke Bay Adventures

Steve Birkinbine is the heart and soul of Auke Bay Adventures. With decades of experience navigating the waters of Southeast Alaska's Inside Passage, Steve brings unmatched expertise, local knowledge, and infectious enthusiasm to every charter.

Guests consistently describe Steve as one of the finest people they've ever met — knowledgeable, flexible, funny, and genuinely dedicated to making each trip exceptional. He knows not just the waters and the fishing, but the history and ecology of the entire region.

When Steve is aboard as your skipper, you're in capable, experienced hands — whether you're chasing halibut, navigating toward a glacier, or watching humpbacks breach at sunrise.
